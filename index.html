About Galiakotwala Engineering Division

Experience. Reputation. Quality.

The Galiakotwala Engineering Company (GEC) is a part of the Galiakotwala Group of Companies. Founded in 1923 as a small hardware trading firm, we have expanded into multiple businesses spanning various geographies across the globe as diversified as cotton trading, industrial and decorative paints, steel packaging containers, chemical plant equipment and property development. Along with geographical reach across India through offices in 23 cities.

GEC provides clients with a complete solution including Design, Fabrication, and Installation Supervision. We have supplied to 175+ Sulphuric Acid plants and many other chemical process plants in various industries across 30 countries. GEC has a state-of-the-art fabrication unit with advanced manufacturing techniques with strong emphasis on quality control, where equipment is manufactured from corrosion resistant CorFre (SX) and many other special Alloys for more than 35 years. We also represent many international Sulphuric Acid plant and equipment suppliers.

MIST ELIMINATORS PRODUCTS

Fiber Bed (Candle Filters)

GEC offers advanced 3rd Generation fiber bed mist eliminators (candle filters) that are used for gas/liquid separation for particles in the range of 0.1 microns and larger. GEC filters has supplied 4000+ mist eliminators across the world.

Mesh (Demister) Pads

GEC offers Speciality mesh pads using advanced technology and materials such as, Co-knit mesh pad (99% efficiency over 2 micron) and CorFre(SX) mesh pads (lasts 3-5 times longer than conventional materials).

Vane Separators

GEC Vane Separators provide low maintenance, product purity, and low pressure drop and efficient handling of solids. GEC has supplied Vane Separators to major chemical groups can manufacture from a whole range of materials.

SULPHURIC ACID EQUIPMENT

Acid Distributors

GEC offers Acid Distributors (Pipe and Trough type) for Drying/Interpass/Final Towers in Sulphuric Acid plants with advanced design and advanced materials. These Distributors have low corrosion, high efficiency (> 42 points/sqm) and low maintenance.

Acid Coolers (Heat Exchangers)

GEC coolers offers Heat Exchangers for Sulphuric and Phosphoric Acid plants with advanced design and material, low maintenance, compact design allowing reduced piping. GEC has supplied more than 100+ Heat Exchangers across the world.

Alloy Acid Piping

GEC offers Acid Piping from advanced alloy materials with significantly low corrosion and long life. GEC team will optimize piping circuits and provide best life cycle value to chemical plants. GEC piping operates in 50+ Acid plants across the world.

Acid Tower

GEC offers Alloy or Brick-Lined Drying/Interpass/Final Acid Towers with complete solution including Design, Fabrication, and Supervision. GEC has Acid Towers achieved 99.99 % absorption efficiency with very low exit mist loads.

Vortex Breaker

We offer Vortex breakers which help to breaks the Vortex. Vortex Breakers can be combined with strainers and nozzles and supplied as one piece of equipment.

Alloy Strainer

We offers inline strainers and strainers for side and bottom outlet nozzles made from mesh and perforated plate.Our strainer offer minimize plugging, easy to clean and last longer.
